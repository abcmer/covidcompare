About Us

Why this map?

Almost all the Covid19 maps we've seen show just the absolute number of cases in a location, but that's not the most important metric. 50 cases in Kentucky isn't the same as 50 cases in NYC.

We pull in data on population, hospital beds, and neighboring counties/states to show the relative risk level so that people can better understand what their community is up against.

Who we are:

A group of volunteers of trying to do our best to provide access to covid19 at the most local level possible

We think county level infection data and state level testing data should be made widely available. Our work is open source and you may use any of the code or data for your own projects.

See our GitHub Page for more details

Disclaimer:

This project is under development and changing rapidly. The risk values shown are currently arbitrary calculations and, while they do represent the relative risk between counties and states, they are not ready to be relied on for any form of decision making.
